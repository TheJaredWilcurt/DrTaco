                  England - 1817 - Mary Shelley's Haunted Mansion - Spooky Spooky Horror Level
              First stage: You time travel in by the front door on the bottom floor of the mansion and move right to proceed. You work your way across fighting Hot Dog Ghosts.
              At the end of the first floor there is a stair case that leads up and to the left, just after it is Mary Shelley's Science lab.

              DR TACO: What's going on here

              MARY: Hot dog monsters have been invading. I was experimenting on the body of one to discover a way to stop them.

              MARY: During my experiments with electricity I unintentionally reanimated the monster FrankenFurter, but upon returning to life he fled upstairs.

              MARY: Worse though, it seems due to the eletrical output of my insturments during the experiment a parabolic temporal flux caused a phase specturm disruption.

              DR TACO: ...I have no idea what that means

              MARY: There are ghosts all over the place!

              DR TACO: Oh... Yeah, I noticed that on my way in. Any idea how do we get rid of them.

              MARY: I've just finished working on a device that can perform a total protonic reversal.

              DR TACO: Uh huh... sooooooo

              MARY: It stops ghosts

              DR TACO: Got it! Okay, I think I can handle that, how do I use it?

              MARY: Ha! Why would I trust it with you, I don't even know your name.

              DR TACO: I'm Doctor Taco.

              MARY: Mary Shelley.

              Mary use a proton pack (ghost busters) to assist you on your ascent. Each floor is a new stage with different enemies and wholes in the floor.
              After reaching the top you walk in on a conversation between BratWORST and Frankenfurter.

              BRATWORST: You're worthless Frankenfurter! You're not a real monster. Do you even have desires of world domination.

              FRANKENFURTER: I don't want to hurt anyone, I just want to be left alone.

              BRATWORST: Disgrace, I can't allow you to besmirch the reputation of the great hot dog empire.

              BratWORST punches Frankenfurter knocking him down

              MARY: Hey, leave him alone! Who do you think you are?

              BRATWORST: I'm BratWORST and I only take orders from King Corndog.

              FRANKENFURTER: He's right, I can't fight him... too strong, just run

              MARY: Come on Frank, don't listen to him, you just need a little help.

              Mary flips the dial forward on her proton pack, a charge sound is made and then she fires electricity at Frankenfurter to charge him up.
              He stands up quickly and knocks BratWORST down. The fight begins with BratWORST stunned. The gameplay mechanic here involves Mary zapping Frankenfurter
              to charge him up, he knocks out BratWORST and then you're able to take his health down before he comes back up and rages.

              After the fight:

              MARY: Let's get out of here Frank, I think it's time we start new lives. Don't know what I'll do for work now though.

              DR TACO: Maybe you should take up writing.

              MARY: Not a bad idea, honestly though, I could use a vacation.

              FRANKENFURTER: I've always wanted to see Geneva.

              DR TACO: Good luck you guys.
